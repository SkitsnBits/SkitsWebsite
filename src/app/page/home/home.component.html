Welcome to Skits'N'Bits! We are a creative‚ fun‚ and welcoming 18+ improv group open to all skill levels․ We serve as a hub for skits‚ projects‚ and other VR performative collaborations!

Workshops

Ready to have fun and learn about improv? Join our weekly workshops on the 1st and 3rd Saturday of the month at 6:00 PM EST.

Shows

Feeling confident in your skills and ready to show off? Apply to our monthly show that occurs every 4th Saturday at 6:00 PM EST.

Jams

Learning is fun and all but sometimes you need a chance to refine what you learn. Join our Jam every 2nd Saturday at 6:00 PM EST.

Films

On occasion we will create a short film that is a bit more planned out. So sit back, relax, grab your popcorn, and enjoy the show.
